ABOUT US

Ek Thal is a food-sharing platform built to tackle two major issues: food waste and hunger. In Nepal, large amounts of edible food are thrown away every day especially during festivals and social gatherings while many people still struggle to afford a proper meal.

With Ek Thal, anyone who has surplus food can quickly post details such as the type of food, quantity, freshness, and location. People in need daily wage workers, students, or NGOs can easily browse nearby listings and claim the food before it spoils.
